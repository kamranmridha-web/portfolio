ICT Fundamentals for SSC Students

Comprehensive training based on the national curriculum for SSC success.

Course Description

This course is designed to provide SSC students with a complete understanding of Information and Communication Technology based on the national curriculum. It builds a strong foundation from basic computer knowledge to practical digital skills that students need for exams and real-life use.

What You Will Learn

  • ✓ Introduction to ICT and its importance in daily life
  • ✓ Basic components of a computer system (hardware and software)
  • ✓ Operating system fundamentals and file management
  • ✓ Word processing, spreadsheet, and presentation tools
  • ✓ Internet usage, email, and online communication
  • ✓ Cyber safety, digital security, and responsible use of technology
  • ✓ Introduction to programming concepts and problem solving

Course Features

✦ Chapter-wise structured lessons
✦ Easy explanations with examples
✦ Practice questions & model tests
✦ Hands-on skills activities
✦ Regular progress assessments

Who is this for?

• SSC students preparing for ICT exams
• Beginners seeking digital literacy
• Students wanting practical knowledge

Expected Outcome

By the end of this course, students will be confident in using computers, understand core ICT concepts, and be well-prepared for their SSC examinations.
