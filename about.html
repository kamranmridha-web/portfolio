// who i am

Shaping Minds Through
Technology & Education

I am Kamran Mridha Raaj, an ICT Lecturer at Educare Ideal School and College in Bangladesh. My mission is simple: make technology accessible, understandable, and exciting for every student.

With over five years of experience in the classroom, I've developed a teaching philosophy that blends theory with hands-on practice. I believe that every student has the potential to understand and harness the power of digital tools — they just need the right guide.

Beyond the classroom, I'm an active developer. I build educational web applications, open-source tools, and digital resources that directly support student learning. My work sits at the intersection of education and technology.

I'm also a firm advocate for digital literacy in Bangladesh, regularly contributing to workshops, community events, and online content that promotes safe, responsible, and empowered technology use.
